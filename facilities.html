Facilities

Laboratories

Biology Lab

A laboratory for biological research and also a workplace for the conduct of scientific research. The most effective vehicle by which the process of inquiry can be learned appears to be our laboratory, where the student experiences, first-hand, the inquiry process. Our Lab provide opportunities for students to model best practices in the study of biology, including application of scientific methodologies, respect for life and the environment, inclusion of learners of all abilities, and consistent adherence to safety standards.

Physics Lab

Well equipped Physics lab to learn in systematic and scientific way. More than a mere place in the back of the classroom, our laboratory is the place where physics students do physics. It is in the laboratory that our students learn to practice the activities of scientists - asking questions, performing procedures, collecting data, analyzing data, answering questions, and thinking of new questions to explore.

Chemistry Lab

The chemistry lab provides a dynamic learning environment that is student centered and curriculum-driven. The floor plan is designed for conversation, collaboration and discovery. The lab contains multiple spaces that provide for long-term multidisciplinary projects, individual and small group learning, inquiry lessons, project-based learning, and problem solving. Flexibility in the arrangement of space, which includes movable workstations, is highlighted one. Universal design allows students with interest to fully participate and have access to all facilities, technology, and safety equipment.

Computer Lab

The Computer lab in our school is designed to accommodate 40 children at a time with the latest flat monitor computer systems and centralized air conditioning along with WiFi connection.
